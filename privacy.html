Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 23, 2026

The short version: this tool needs no account and no signup. We don't store your bucket lists anywhere — they live entirely in your own browser and in the link you save. The only personal info we ever see is what you choose to give us through the optional feedback or contact form.

What we collect

  • Nothing, by default. Generating, editing, checking off, and saving a list all happen in your browser. We never see your list content.
  • Your email and message — only if you voluntarily submit the feedback box or contact form.
  • Basic, anonymous analytics — page visits and which buttons get used, via a privacy-focused, cookieless analytics tool. It doesn't identify you personally.

Your saved list link

When you tap "Save my list," your list is encoded directly into the URL itself — it's never sent to or stored on any server. Anyone with that exact link can view that list, so treat it like you would any link you don't want shared publicly.

How we use what we collect

Feedback and contact messages are used only to respond to you and improve the tool. Analytics are used only to understand, in aggregate, how the tool is used — not to track individuals.

Who we share it with

We don't sell or share your information. A couple of trusted services help run the site behind the scenes:

  • Formspree — delivers the feedback/contact form submissions to us.
  • Netlify — hosts the website.
  • GoatCounter — provides anonymous, cookieless traffic analytics.

If you arrived here through an ad, the advertising platform (such as Meta) collected its own data about that ad interaction, governed by its own privacy policy — separately from anything on this page.

Cookies

This site doesn't use tracking or advertising cookies.

Children

This site is intended for general audiences and doesn't knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes

If this policy changes, we'll update the date at the top of this page.

Questions

Reach out anytime via the contact page.

This is a plain-language summary, not formal legal advice.
